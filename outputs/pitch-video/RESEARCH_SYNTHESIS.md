## What The Mezo Pitch Workshop Changes

The pitch cannot be a technical tour. The workshop's strongest guidance is:

- Open with the core sentence in under 15 seconds.
- Tell a business story, not a feature list.
- Show why the team is credible, because evaluators back teams as much as ideas.
- Name one clear problem. Do not list twenty.
- Explain the solution from the end-user point of view.
- Keep the demo short and choose only the most valuable proof.
- Quantify the problem or opportunity when possible.
- Mention competition or alternatives instead of pretending none exist.
- Share early traction or proof if available.
- End with a clear ask.
- Rehearse until the story is automatic.
- Use AI for critique, but keep a distinctive style instead of generic AI copy.

## What The Motion Council Changes

The video should use Remotion as the deterministic assembly layer. Final UI text,
captions, proof labels, numbers, and CTA must be rendered in Remotion, not by AI
video tools. Real app recordings become the truth layer. AI inserts are now
integrated only as short texture shots at the cold open, demo transition, and
final sting.

The cut follows the eight-beat spine:

1. Hook
2. Tension
3. Promise
4. Demo
5. Proof
6. Payoff / business viability
7. CTA / ask
8. Sting

The council also requires the three-texture rule:

- Deterministic motion graphics: Remotion type, gauges, proof cards.
- Real captured texture: final app recordings to be inserted later.
- Source texture: short AI-generated abstract clips used as cinematic texture,
  never as product evidence.

## Pitch Strategy For Borealis

Core sentence:

> Borealis is a MEZO-powered MUSD capital router for the Mezo ecosystem.

Problem:

> Mezo can create productive Bitcoin-backed capital, but ecosystem teams still
> need a transparent way to request, prioritize, and verify MUSD incentive flows.

Solution:

> Borealis lets projects apply for gauges, lets MEZO-powered signals rank those
> gauges, and lets ecosystem operators verify MUSD allocation decisions through
> Mezo testnet contract and transaction proof.

Winning angle:

> This is not just a dashboard. It is a reusable ecosystem primitive for the
> MEZO Utilization track: MEZO gets a visible job, MUSD gets measurable flow,
> and builders get an accountable route to capital.

## Design Constraints

- Do not show final app footage until it is recorded from the real working app.
- Do not invent mainnet claims.
- Do not use AI-generated UI as product proof.
- Keep AI footage symbolic: capital flow, gauges, and ecosystem texture only.
- Keep each screen sparse. Every word must earn space.
- Repeat "Borealis" and the core sentence at the start and end.
- Demo segment must contain explicit placeholders for future recordings.
